Meet Janice, Spider Savior!

Itsy was a spider hero. He cleans rain gutters for a homeless shelter. He was dedicated to helping Portland's most unfortunate. During our latest rainstorm, a burst of water came down the spout and washed pour Itsy out. He suffered serious injuries and was not able to climb up the spout again.

Itsy, permanently disabled, needed a home. Many people hate spiders and are not willing to take care of them. Itsy was suffering from spider discrimination.

Via Spider Saviors, Janice graciously volunteered to be Itsy's hero and foster him. Spider Saviors helped Itsy find a loving home, where he will be taken care of by Janice. Janice is a Spider Savior!
